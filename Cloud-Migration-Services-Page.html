  • Security implementation

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cloud migration services.

The timeline for cloud migration varies depending on the complexity and scale of your infrastructure. A small business with a few applications might take 4-6 weeks, while enterprise-level migrations can take 3-6 months or more. During our initial assessment, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your needs.

We design our migration strategies to minimize downtime. In many cases, we can implement a parallel migration approach where your existing systems remain operational while we build and test the cloud environment. The final cutover typically requires only minimal downtime, often scheduled during off-hours to reduce business impact. For critical systems, we can implement high-availability solutions to ensure continuous operation.

Security is our top priority throughout the migration process. We implement multiple layers of protection, including data encryption in transit and at rest, secure VPN connections, access controls, and continuous monitoring. We follow industry best practices and compliance standards (HIPAA, PCI DSS, etc.) relevant to your business. After migration, we implement ongoing security measures such as vulnerability scanning, intrusion detection, and regular security audits to protect your cloud environment.

Yes, we have extensive experience migrating legacy applications to the cloud. Depending on the application, we may recommend different approaches: rehosting (lift and shift), replatforming (making cloud-optimized adjustments), or in some cases, refactoring/rearchitecting for optimal cloud performance. During our assessment phase, we'll evaluate your legacy applications and recommend the most appropriate migration strategy for each one, considering factors like compatibility, performance requirements, and long-term maintenance.

We implement comprehensive rollback plans for every migration to ensure business continuity. Before any major migration step, we create backups and establish recovery points. If an issue arises, we can quickly revert to the previous state while we resolve the problem. Our team monitors the migration process in real-time, allowing us to identify and address potential issues before they impact your operations. Additionally, we maintain your original environment until the migration is fully validated and stable.
